"Aymos (Babili Amos Shili, born January 17, 1995) is a South African singer and songwriter from Tembisa, Gauteng. He is known for blending traditional sounds with modern Amapiano music.\n\nHis breakout hit single is 'Emcimbini'. He has collaborated with artists such as DJ Maphorisa and Kabza de Small. Some of his other popular songs include 'Partii' and 'Sengizwile'.\n\nHe released the collaborative EP 'Shonamalanga', which was nominated for Best Amapiano Album and Best Duo/Group at the South African Music Awards (SAMAs). Aymos's net worth is expected to increase as he continues to solidify his career."
